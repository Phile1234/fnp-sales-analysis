# Ferns and Petals Sales Analysis

This project analyzes sales data from Ferns and Petals (FNP), a gift delivery company that serves occasions such as Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Valentine's Day, Birthdays, and Anniversaries.

## Objective

The goal of this analysis is to uncover useful business insights related to:
- sales trends
- customer behavior
- product performance
- delivery efficiency

## Key Business Questions

This project answers the following questions:
1. What is the total revenue?
2. What is the average order and delivery time?
3. How did monthly sales perform across 2023?
4. Which products generated the highest revenue?
5. What is the average customer spending?
6. How did the top 5 products perform in sales?
7. Which are the top 10 cities by number of orders?
8. Does higher order quantity affect delivery time?
9. How does revenue compare across different occasions?
10. Which products are most popular during specific occasions?
